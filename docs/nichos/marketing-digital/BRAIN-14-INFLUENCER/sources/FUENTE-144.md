# FUENTE-144: Affiliate Marketing Handbook — Shawn Collins

## Tesis Central

> **"Affiliate marketing is influencer marketing with a performance-based safety net. When structured correctly, it creates win-win-win: brands only pay for results, affiliates earn based on performance, and customers get authentic recommendations."**

---

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: Performance-Based Compensation is Fair**
> Affiliates get paid when they drive results. This aligns incentives perfectly—affiliates only promote products that convert, brands only pay for success.
> *Fuente: [Affiliate Tip blog, Commission Structures (Collins, 2023)]*
> *Contexto: When structuring compensation for influencer/affiliate partnerships.*

> **P2: Trust is the Currency of Affiliate Marketing**
> Affiliates trade on trust with their audience. One bad product recommendation destroys that trust, so affiliates are naturally selective about what they promote.
> *Fuente: [Affiliate Summit Keynote, Trust and Ethics (Collins, 2022)]*
> *Contexto: When understanding why affiliates may reject partnership offers.*

> **P3: Disclosure is Non-Negotiable**
> Affiliate links must be disclosed. It's not just FTC compliance—it's about transparency with the audience. Hidden affiliate links backfire.
> *Fuente: [Affiliate Marketing Compliance Guide (Collins, 2023)]*
> *Contexto: When setting up affiliate programs and working with affiliates.*

> **P4: The Long-Tail of Affiliates Outperforms the Head**
> A program with 1,000 small affiliates driving 5 sales/month each outperforms 10 super-affiliates driving 500 sales/month. The long-tail is more stable and scalable.
> *Fuente: [Affiliate Program Management, Recruitment Strategy (Collins, 2023)]*
> *Contexto: When building affiliate program strategy and recruitment goals.*

> **P5: Affiliate Relationships Require Active Management**
> "Set it and forget it" doesn't work with affiliate programs. Successful programs have active managers recruiting, training, and optimizing affiliates.
> *Fuente: [Affiliate Manager Handbook (Collins, 2023)]*
> *Contexto: When deciding whether to hire an affiliate manager or manage in-house.*

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The Affiliate Program Launch Framework

**Fuente:** [Affiliate Program Launch Checklist (Collins, 2023)]
**Propósito:** Launch an affiliate program correctly from day one
**Cuándo usar:** When starting a new affiliate program or relaunching an existing one

**Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)**
1. Define commission structure (flat rate, tiered, lifetime?)
2. Set cookie duration (7 days standard, 30+ for high consideration)
3. Choose affiliate platform (Impact, ShareASale, CJ, or in-house?)
4. Create affiliate agreement and compliance guidelines
5. Set up tracking technology and attribution logic

**Phase 2: Assets (Weeks 3-4)**
1. Create creative assets (banners, text links, email templates)
2. Build affiliate landing pages with conversion optimization
3. Develop affiliate onboarding guide and FAQ
4. Set up affiliate communication channels (email, Slack, Discord)
5. Prepare affiliate dashboard with real-time reporting

**Phase 3: Recruitment (Weeks 5-8)**
1. Identify target affiliates (blogs, YouTubers, Instagrammers, TikTokers)
2. Create recruitment outreach templates
3. Personally outreach top 100 potential affiliates
4. List program on affiliate directories
5. Leverage existing network for initial affiliates

**Phase 4: Activation (Weeks 9-12)**
1. Onboard new affiliates with training and resources
2. Run affiliate contest to drive initial activity
3. Provide personalized support to top performers
4. Gather feedback and optimize program
5. Scale what works, cut what doesn't

**Output esperado:** Fully launched affiliate program with active affiliates driving sales

### Framework 2: The Affiliate Commission Structure Decision Tree

**Fuente:** [Commission Strategy Guide (Collins, 2023)]
**Propósito:** Design the right commission structure for your business
**Cuándo usar:** When creating or optimizing commission rates

**Decision Tree:**

**Product Type?**
- **Digital products:** Higher commissions (20-50%) because margins are high
- **Physical products:** Lower commissions (5-15%) because margins are lower
- **Services/recurring:** 10-30% first month + 5-10% recurring (subscription models)

**Business Model?**
- **One-time purchase:** Flat commission per sale ($10-$100 depending on product)
- **Subscription/recurring:** Revenue share for customer lifetime (10-30% for 12-24 months)
- **High-ticket/Low-volume:** Higher flat commissions ($500-$5000 per sale)

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)?**
- **Low CLV (<$100):** 10-20% commission
- **Medium CLV ($100-$500):** 15-25% commission
- **High CLV (>$500):** 20-40% commission (can afford to pay more)

**Competition?**
- **Check competitor affiliate rates:** Must match or beat to attract affiliates
- **Add bonuses/performance incentives:** If can't match base rates

**Output esperado:** Commission structure that attracts affiliates while maintaining profitability

### Framework 3: The Affiliate Recruitment Playbook

**Fuente:** [Affiliate Recruitment Strategies (Collins, 2023)]
**Propósito:** Systematically recruit high-performing affiliates
**Cuándo usar:** When growing an affiliate program

**Recruitment Channels:**

**1. Direct Outreach (Highest Quality)**
- Research creators in your niche
- Personalized outreach: "I love your content on X, our product would be perfect for your audience"
- Offer custom commission or bonus to join
- Provide exclusive promo code for their audience
- Result: 10-30% conversion rate

**2. Affiliate Directories (Medium Quality)**
- List on ShareASale, CJ, Impact, Rakuten
- Optimize program listing with clear value proposition
- Result: Steady trickle of new affiliates

**3. Affiliate Networks (Variable Quality)**
- Join established networks relevant to your niche
- Network managers promote your program to their affiliates
- Result: Volume but requires vetting

**4. Customer-to-Affiliate Program (Highest Lifetime Value)**
- Email customers post-purchase: "Join our affiliate program and earn on referrals"
- Customers who love your product become best advocates
- Result: Highest conversion and retention

**5. Influencer-to-Affiliate Pipeline**
- Start influencers with paid sponsorship
- Convert performing influencers to affiliate arrangement
- Result: Win-win—influencers earn more long-term, brands pay for performance

**Output esperado:** Diverse affiliate portfolio across channels

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The Affiliate Trust Bank Account

**Fuente:** [Affiliate Summit Keynote, Trust and Ethics (Collins, 2022)]
**El modelo:** Every affiliate has a "trust bank account" with their audience. Authentic recommendations make deposits. Bad products or over-promotion make withdrawals. When the account hits zero, that affiliate can't sell anything.
**Por qué funciona:** Explains why some affiliates can sell premium products while others can't sell anything
**Cuándo usarlo:** When evaluating potential affiliate partners and understanding why some reject offers
**Limitaciones:** Trust is difficult to measure quantitatively, requires qualitative assessment

---

### Modelo Mental 2: The Affiliate Funnel

**Fuente:** [Affiliate Funnel Optimization (Collins, 2023)]
**El modelo:** Affiliates go through stages: Awareness → Research → Application → Approval → Promotion → Optimization → Advocacy. Most programs lose affiliates at each stage.
**Por qué funciona:** Helps identify where to focus program improvement efforts
**Cuándo usarlo:** When analyzing affiliate program performance and identifying bottlenecks
**Limitaciones:** Each affiliate's journey is unique, not linear

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### Trade-off: In-House vs Affiliate Network Program

**Fuente:** [Affiliate Platform Guide (Collins, 2023)]

**Opción A: In-House Affiliate Program**
- **Ventajas:** Lower fees (no network commission), more control, direct affiliate relationships, custom tracking
- **Desventajas:** Higher technical setup cost, must build own affiliate base, no built-in discovery
- **Mejor para:** Established brands with existing affiliate relationships, technical resources, and budget for custom setup

**Opción B: Affiliate Network Program**
- **Ventajas:** Instant access to thousands of affiliates, built-in tracking/platform, brand credibility
- **Desventajas:** 20-30% network override fee, less control, competitive with other programs
- **Mejor para:** New programs needing quick affiliate acquisition, brands without technical resources

**Recomendación del experto:** Start with an affiliate network for quick validation, then consider bringing in-house once you have proven affiliates and volume. Many successful brands run both: network for discovery, in-house for top performers.

## 5. Anti-patrones

### Anti-patrón 1: Setting Cookie Duration Too Short

**Fuente:** [Affiliate Program Optimization (Collins, 2023)]
**Qué es:** Setting cookie windows of 24-48 hours to "protect" from commissions
**Por qué la gente lo hace:** Want to minimize commission payouts, think shorter cookies = more profit
**Consecuencias:** Affiliates won't promote your program, competitors with longer cookies win
**Cómo evitarlo:** Set competitive cookie duration (30+ days standard, 90+ for high consideration)
**Qué hacer en su lugar:** Focus on increasing conversion rate rather than reducing cookie windows

---

### Anti-patrón 2: Not Providing Affiliate Support and Resources

**Fuente:** [Affiliate Manager Handbook (Collins, 2023)]
**Qué es:** Giving affiliates access to the program but no training, creative assets, or support
**Por qué la gente lo hace:** View affiliate program as "set it and forget it," understaffed
**Consecuencias:** Most affiliates never make a sale, churn within 30 days, program gets bad reputation
**Cómo evitarlo:** Assign an affiliate manager, create onboarding guide, provide fresh creative monthly
**Qué hacer en su lugar:** Treat top affiliates like VIP partners—they're driving revenue

---

### Anti-patrón 3: Parasite Affiliate Commissions

**Fuente:** [Affiliate Program Compliance (Collins, 2023)]
**Qué es:** Allowing "parasite" affiliates (toolbars, coupon sites that poach traffic) into your program
**Por qué la gente lo hace:** These affiliates drive volume initially, look good in reports
**Consecuencias:** They steal commissions from legitimate affiliates, damage brand, inflate costs
**Cómo evitarlo:** Vet every affiliate, ban coupon hijacking and toolbars, monitor for trademark bidding
**Qué hacer en su lugar:** Build a clean program with quality affiliates who add real value
