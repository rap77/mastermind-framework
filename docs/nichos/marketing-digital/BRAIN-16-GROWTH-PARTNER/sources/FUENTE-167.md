# FUENTE-167: Agency Operations — Operations Framework

## Tesis Central

> **"Great strategy with poor execution fails. Great execution with average strategy succeeds. Agency operations are the engine that turns strategy into results consistently at scale."**

---

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: Systems Replace Heroics**
- Rely on systems, not heroes. When your star employee leaves, the system continues.
> *Contexto: When building agency operations and processes.*

> **P2: Standardization Enables Scalability**
- If you can't document it, you can't scale it. Standard operating procedures (SOPs) are non-negotiable.
> *Contexto: When designing agency delivery systems.*

> **P3: Quality Assurance is Not Optional**
- Quality doesn't happen by accident. Systematic QA prevents client churn and reputation damage.
> *Contexto: When building agency quality systems.*

> **P4: Project Management is the Glue**
- Without PM, agencies are chaos. PM is the discipline that delivers on time, on budget, on spec.
> *Contexto: When structuring agency delivery management.*

---

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The Agency Operations Maturity Model

**Fuente:** [Agency Operations Framework (Ops Experts, 2023)]

**Level 1: Ad-Hoc (Founder-Run)**
- **Systems:** In founder's head, tribal knowledge
- **Processes:** React to client needs, no planning
- **Quality:** Variable, depends on heroics
- **Scalability:** Limited to founder's capacity

**Level 2: Defined (Basic Systems)**
- **Systems:** Basic SOPs for common tasks
- **Processes:** Project-based, reactive but organized
- **Quality:** Basic QA, checklists
- **Scalability:** Can grow to $1-2M with pain

**Level 3: Optimized (Systematic)**
- **Systems:** Comprehensive SOPs, knowledge base
- **Processes:** Proactive planning, resource management
- **Quality:** Systematic QA, metrics, continuous improvement
- **Scalability:** Can grow to $5-10M with consistency

**Level 4: Scalable (Automated)**
- **Systems:** Automated workflows, AI augmentation
- **Processes:** Predictable, optimized, data-driven
- **Quality:** Predictable quality, client satisfaction monitored
- **Scalability:** Can grow to $50M+ with quality

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The Operations-Strategy Link

**El modelo:** Strategy without operations is hallucination. Operations without strategy is organized chaos. Growth partners need both at high levels.
**Por qué funciona:** Explains why agencies with brilliant strategy but poor ops fail to scale

---

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### Trade-off: Customization vs. Standardization

**Recomendación:** Standardize 80% of delivery, customize 20%. This balances efficiency with client needs.
