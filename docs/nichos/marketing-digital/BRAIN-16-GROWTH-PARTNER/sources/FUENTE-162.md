# FUENTE-162: Agency Profitability — Marcel Petitpas

## Tesis Central

> **"Most agencies aren't profitable because they price based on costs and competitors, not value. The journey from cost-plus to value-based pricing is the journey from struggling vendor to profitable growth partner."**

---

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: Profit is Not What Remains, It's What You Design For**
- Most agencies hope for profit. Value-based agencies design for profit from the start.
> *Contexto: When assessing agency financial health.*

> **P2: Gross Margin is the Leading Indicator of Agency Health**
- If your gross margin (revenue - delivery cost) isn't 50%+, you have a broken business model.
> *Contexto: When evaluating agency unit economics.*

> **P3: Client Profitability Varies Wildly**
- Your best clients are 3-5x more profitable than your worst clients. Many agencies lose money on 20% of clients.
> *Contexto: When assessing client portfolio and pricing.*

> **P4: The Value Conversation is Hard but Necessary**
- Talking about money and value feels uncomfortable. That's why you should hire people good at it.
> *Contexto: When building agency sales capabilities.*

---

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The Agency Profitability Audit

**Fuente:** [Agency Profitability Framework (Petitpas, 2023)]

**Step 1: Calculate Gross Margin per Client**
- **Revenue:** What client pays
- **Direct Costs:** Team time directly billable to client
- **Gross Margin:** Revenue - Direct Costs
- **Target:** 50%+ gross margin

**Step 2: Calculate Client Profitability**
- **Gross Margin:** From Step 1
- **Overhead Allocation:** Fair share of agency overhead
- **Net Profit:** Gross Margin - Overhead
- **Target:** 20%+ net profit per client

**Step 3: Client Segment Analysis**
- **Tier A (Most Profitable):** Top 20% of clients by profit
- **Tier B (Moderate):** Middle 60% of clients
- **Tier C (Unprofitable):** Bottom 20% (losing money)

**Step 4: Action by Tier**
- **Tier A:** Raise prices, expand services, replicate characteristics
- **Tier B:** Improve profitability through efficiency or pricing
- **Tier C:** Fire or raise prices significantly

---

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The Profitability Tiers

**El modelo:** Agency profitability follows power law: Top 20% of clients drive 80% of profit. Bottom 20% lose money. The middle 60% are mediocre.
**Por qué funciona:** Explains why revenue growth without profitability analysis is dangerous

---

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### Trade-off: Revenue Growth vs. Profitability

**Recomendación:** Never sacrifice gross margin for revenue. Growth with poor margins is a trap.
