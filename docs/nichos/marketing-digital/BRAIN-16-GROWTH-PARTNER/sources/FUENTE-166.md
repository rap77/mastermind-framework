# FUENTE-166: Client Retention — Gainsight Framework

## Tesis Central

> **"Acquisition gets you customers, retention keeps them. The most expensive customer is the one you have to keep replacing. Customer success is the foundation of sustainable growth partnerships."**

---

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: Customer Success is Proactive, Not Reactive**
- Don't wait for churn signals. Engage proactively to ensure success.
> *Contexto: When designing customer success strategy.*

> **P2: Health Scores Predict Churn**
- Every customer has a health score. Monitor it, act on it, prevent churn before it happens.
> *Contexto: When designing client monitoring and intervention.*

> **P3: Onboarding is the Most Critical Phase**
- The first 90 days determine long-term retention. Nail onboarding or lose them later.
> *Contexto: When designing client onboarding and success planning.*

> **P4: Expansion Revenue is Cheaper than New Logo Revenue**
- Selling more to existing customers is 5-10x cheaper than acquiring new ones.
> *Contexto: When measuring agency growth and client development.*

---

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The Customer Health Score Model

**Fuente:** [Customer Success Framework (Gainsight, 2023)]

**Health Score Components:**

**1. Product Engagement (40%)**
- **Usage Frequency:** Daily, weekly, monthly usage
- **Feature Adoption:** % of features actively used
- **Depth of Use:** Advanced features vs basic
- **Growth Trend:** Increasing or decreasing usage

**2. Relationship Health (30%)**
- **NPS/CSAT:** Satisfaction scores
- **Executive Sponsorship:** Do we have champion at client?
- **Communication Quality:** Responsiveness, engagement depth
- **Strategic Alignment:** Are we aligned on goals?

**3. Business Outcomes (30%)**
- **Goal Achievement:** Are they achieving their goals?
- **ROI Realization:** Are they seeing expected value?
- **Referenceability:** Would they recommend us?

**Health Score Calculation:**
- **0-30:** Red - At risk, immediate intervention
- **31-70:** Yellow - Monitor, proactive engagement
- **71-100:** Green - Healthy, expansion opportunities

**Actions by Score:**
- **Red:** Account executive outreach, success plan intervention, executive review
- **Yellow:** Customer success outreach, QBR, success plan review
- **Green:** Expansion conversation, ask for referral, case study

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The Churn Curve

**El modelo:** Churn risk follows a U-curve: highest in first 90 days (onboarding), drops in middle, rises again at 18-24 months if not refreshed.
**Por qué funciona:** Explains when to focus retention efforts for maximum impact

---

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### Trade-off: Acquisition vs. Retention Investment

**Recommendation:** Early-stage agencies focus 70% acquisition / 30% retention. Mature agencies shift to 50/50 or 40/60. Growth partners maximize retention and expansion.
