# FUENTE-163: Agency Productization — Brent Weaver

## Tesis Central

> **"The service agency model is broken—unscalable, low-margin, feast-or-famine. Productized services fix this: fixed scope, fixed price, fixed process, scalable delivery. That's the foundation for becoming a growth partner."**

---

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: Productized Services are Scalable, Custom Services are Not**
- If you can't describe it, price it, and deliver it the same way every time, it's not productized.
> *Contexto: When assessing agency scalability and maturity.*

> **P2: The Problem with "We Can Do Anything"**
- Generalist agencies are commodities. Specialist productized services are premium.
> *Contexto: When evaluating agency positioning and differentiation.*

> **P3: Your Process is Your Product**
- In productized services, the methodology is as valuable as the outcome.
> *Contexto: When designing agency offerings and client value proposition.*

---

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The Productized Service Canvas

**Fuente:** [Productized Services Blueprint (Weaver, 2023)]

**1. Problem Solved:** What specific business problem do you solve?
**2. Target Client:** Who exactly is this for? (Narrow is better)
**3. Outcome:** What specific outcome do you deliver?
**4. Timeline:** How long does it take? (Fixed)
**5. Process:** What is your repeatable methodology?
**6. Deliverables:** What exactly does the client get? (Clear scope)
**7. Price:** What is the fixed price? (Value-based)
**8. Guarantee:** What do you guarantee? (Risk reversal)

**Example:**
- **Problem:** "Websites don't convert"
- **Target:** E-commerce stores doing $1M+ revenue
- **Outcome:** 30% conversion increase in 90 days
- **Timeline:** 90 days, fixed
- **Process:** 3-phase methodology (Audit → Design → Optimize)
- **Deliverables:** CRO audit, redesigned landing pages, implementation
- **Price:** $30K fixed
- **Guarantee:** 30% increase or we work free until you get it

---

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The Scalability Ceiling

**El modelo:** Custom services have low scalability (linear growth with headcount). Productized services have high scalability (revenue grows faster than headcount).
**Por qué funciona:** Explains why some agencies can scale to $50M while others plateau at $1M

---

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### Trade-off: Custom Services vs. Productized Services

**Productized:**
- **Ventajas:** Scalable, predecible, higher margins
- **Desventajas:** Less flexible, requires marketing

**Custom:**
- **Ventajas:** Flexible, premium pricing
- **Desventajas:** Not scalable, delivery complexity

**Recomendación:** Start with one flagship productized service, add custom for premium clients.
