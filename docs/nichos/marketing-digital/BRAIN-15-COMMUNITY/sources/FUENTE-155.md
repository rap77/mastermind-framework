# FUENTE-155: UGC Strategy — GoPro Team

## Tesis Central

> **"When your customers are your best content creators, you don't need to produce everything yourself. Empower your community to create, share, and be celebrated. Their content is more authentic than anything you could make."**

---

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: Make Customers the Heroes, Not Your Brand**
- GoPro's "Be a Hero" positioned customers as heroes, not GoPro as hero. This shift created endless authentic content.
> *Fuente: [GoPro Marketing Strategy (GoPro, 2023)]*
> *Contexto: When designing UGC campaigns and community content strategy.*

> **P2: Lower the Barrier to Creation**
- The easier it is for members to create content, the more they will. Provide templates, prompts, hashtags, examples.
> *Fuente: [UGC Best Practices (GoPro, 2023)]*
> *Contexto: When designing community content initiatives and contests.*

> **P3: Celebrate and Amplify User Content**
- Nothing motivates more creators than seeing their content amplified by the brand. Feature, reward, celebrate UGC.
> *Fuente: [Content Curation Strategy (GoPro, 2023)]*
> *Contexto: When running UGC programs and managing community content.*

> **P4: Give Credit Generously**
- Always credit creators. Tag them, shout them out, show appreciation. Credit builds loyalty and encourages more creation.
> *Fuente: [Creator Recognition (GoPro, 2023)]*
> *Contexto: When sharing community-created content.*

> **P5: Quality Over Quantity in UGC**
- Better to feature 10 amazing pieces than 100 mediocre ones. Curation builds excitement and aspiration.
> *Fuente: [Content Curation (GoPro, 2023)]*
> *Contexto: When selecting and featuring community content.*

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The UGC Campaign Framework

**Fuente:** [GoPro UGC Playbook (GoPro, 2023)]
**Propósito:** Design and execute successful UGC campaigns
**Cuándo usar:** When launching community content initiatives and contests

**Phase 1: Campaign Design**
1. **Define Theme:** Clear, inspiring prompt (e.g., "Your Best Adventure")
2. **Create Hashtag:** Unique, memorable, branded (#GoProFamily)
3. **Set Guidelines:** What content qualifies, how to enter, deadline
4. **Design Rewards:** Recognition, prizes, features (be generous)
5. **Plan Amplification:** How will winning content be shared?

**Phase 2: Launch and Promotion**
1. **Announce Campaign:** Email, social, in-app notifications
2. **Provide Examples:** Show what great submissions look like
3. **Create Templates:** Make it easy to participate (overlays, music, effects)
4. **Remind and Encourage:** Mid-campaign reminders, early entries showcase
5. **Influencer Amplification:** Partner with creators to kickstart participation

**Phase 3: Collection and Curation**
1. **Monitor Hashtag:** Track all submissions in real-time
2. **Initial Screen:** Remove off-brand, low-quality, or inappropriate content
3. **Community Voting:** Let community help select winners (engagement + fairness)
4. **Final Selection:** Team picks from top-voted submissions
5. **Rights Management:** Secure permissions to use content

**Phase 4: Celebration and Amplification**
1. **Announce Winners:** Big announcement, celebrate all participants
2. **Feature Winning Content:** Website, social, ads, email, product packaging
3. **Creator Spotlights:** Profiles of winning creators, their stories
4. **Post-Campaign Content:** Compilation video, gallery, case study
5. **Next Teaser:** Preview next campaign to keep momentum

**Output esperado:** High-volume UGC campaign with quality submissions and community engagement

---

### Framework 2: The Hashtag Campaign Strategy

**Fuente:** [GoPro Hashtag Strategy (GoPro, 2023)]
**Propósito:** Create hashtag campaigns that generate ongoing UGC
**Cuándo usar:** When designing always-on UGC programs (not just campaigns)

**Hashtag Best Practices:**

**1. Keep it Simple and Memorable**
- **Good:** #GoProFamily, #ShotOniPhone, #MyCalvins
- **Bad:** #GoProUserGeneratedContentCampaign2023
- **Test:** Can someone remember it after hearing once?

**2. Make it Branded but Flexible**
- Include brand name (GoPro)
- Allow creativity (Family, Hero, Adventure)
- Encourage interpretation

**3. Launch with Examples**
- Show 10-20 examples of great content
- Use early adopters to seed the hashtag
- Make it easy to understand "what counts"

**4. Monitor and Moderate**
- Track hashtag volume daily
- Remove spam/off-brand quickly
- Feature great submissions to encourage more

**5. Keep it Evergreen**
- Avoid time-bound language (e.g., "Summer2023")
- Make it a always-on program, not limited campaign
- Refresh prompts but keep hashtag

**Metrics:**
- **Hashtag volume:** Posts per day/week
- **Participation rate:** % of community using hashtag
- **Quality score:** % of submissions that are feature-worthy
- **Amplification:** Featured content views/engagement

**Output esperado:** Ongoing stream of quality UGC through branded hashtag

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The Content Flywheel

**Fuente:** [GoPro Content Strategy (GoPro, 2023)]
**El modelo:** Great UGC gets featured → More creators see they can be featured → More creators submit → More great UGC. The flywheel accelerates or stalls based on how well you amplify and celebrate creators.
**Por qué funciona:** Explains why some brands have endless UGC while others struggle to get any
**Cuándo usarlo:** When diagnosing UGC program performance and planning improvements

---

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### Trade-off: Curated UGC vs Open Submission

**Fuente:** [UGC Strategy Guide (GoPro, 2023)]

**Opción A: Highly Curated**
- **Ventajas:** Only best content, brand-safe, aspirational
- **Desventajas:** Lower participation, exclusivity perception
- **Mejor para:** Premium brands, visual-first products

**Opción B: Open Submission**
- **Ventajas:** Higher participation, inclusive, volume
- **Desventajas:** Variable quality, more moderation needed
- **Mejor para:** Community brands, high-volume products

**Recomendación:** Start open to build participation, then curate for features. Feature the top 5-10% to inspire quality while maintaining inclusivity.

---

## 5. Anti-patrones

### Anti-patrón 1: Using UGC Without Permission

**Qué es:** Reposting user content without asking or crediting
**Consecuencias:** Legal issues, creator backlash, community trust damage
**Qué hacer en su lugar:** Always ask permission, always credit, offer to take down if requested
