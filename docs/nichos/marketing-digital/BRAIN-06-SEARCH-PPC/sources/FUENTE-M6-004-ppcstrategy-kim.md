# FUENTE-M6-004: PPC Strategy — Larry Kim

## Tesis Central

> El éxito en PPC no es about outbidding, es about out-converting. El modelo "Conversion-Centric PPC" significa: every ad, keyword, y landing page debe be optimized para conversion rate primero, CPC segundo. La mayoría de advertisers focusean en driving clicks (vanity metric) en lugar de driving conversions (business metric). La key insight:CTR correlation with conversions is weak. High CTR ≠ High conversion rate. Focus on writing ads that attract BUYERS, not clickers. Use ad copy to qualify prospects, not just capture attention. Better to have fewer clicks but higher conversion rate than many clicks but low conversion rate. SEM es un game de economics: Revenue = (Clicks × Conversion Rate × AOV) - (Clicks × CPC). Optimize Conversion Rate y AOV first, CPC last.

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: Conversion rate beats CPC every time**
- High conversion rate = can afford higher CPC
- Low conversion rate = even low CPC is expensive
- Focus on landing page experience first
> *Fuente: "Conversion First" (Kim, 2021)*

> **P2: Ad copy must qualify, not just capturar**
- "Click here" ads get clicks but no conversions
- Specific ads scare away tire-kickers but attract buyers
- Use disqualification strategically
> *Fuente: "Ad Qualification" (Kim, 2021)*

> **P3: Long-tail keywords = higher conversion rates**
- Specific keywords = higher intent
- Broad keywords = lower intent, more waste
- 70% of budget en long-tail, 30% en head terms
> *Fuente: "Long-Tail Strategy" (Kim, 2020)*

> **P4: Landing page experience es 80% of success**
- Great ad + bad landing page = wasted spend
- Bad ad + great landing page = some conversions
- Test landing page antes de scaling ad spend
> *Fuente: "Landing Page Dominance" (Kim, 2021)*

> **P5: The funnel matters en PPC**
- Not all searches have same purchase intent
- Match offer to search stage (informational vs transactional)
- Don't send all traffic to same page/offer
> *Fuente: "Funnel Matching" (Kim, 2020)*

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The Ad Copy Formula Framework

**Fuente:** "Ad Copy System" (Kim, 2021)
**Propósito:** Escribir ads que conviertan, no solo clickean.
**Cuándo usar:** Al crear Google Search ads.

**Formula A: Problem-Agitation-Solution (PAS)**

**Structure:**
1. **Problem:** Call out the pain point
2. **Agitation:** Make it worse (emotional hook)
3. **Solution:** Your product as the answer

**Example (CRM Software):**
```
Title: Struggling to Close Sales? (Problem)
Title: Stop Losing Leads to Competitors (Agitation)
Title: #1 CRM for Closing Deals (Solution)

Description: Proven CRM system.
Close 3x more deals this month.
Start your free 14-day trial.
```

**Formula B: Before-After-Bridge (BAB)**

**Structure:**
1. **Before:** Describe current painful situation
2. **After:** Describe desired outcome
3. **Bridge:** Your product as the bridge

**Example (Weight Loss):**
```
Title: Tired of Failed Diets? (Before)
Title: Lose Weight Without Starving (After)
Title: Proven System Works (Bridge)

Description: Sustainable weight loss.
10,000+ success stories.
Learn more today.
```

**Formula C: Specific-Number-Result (SNR)**

**Structure:**
1. **Specific:** Be specific about target audience
2. **Number:** Use numbers (proven better than text)
3. **Result:** Clear outcome

**Example (SEO Services):**
```
Title: SEO for Local Businesses (Specific)
Title: 47% More Traffic in 90 Days (Number)
Title: Guaranteed Results (Result)

Description: Proven SEO strategy.
Rank on page 1 of Google.
Get your free audit today.
```

**Key Rule:** Test multiple formulas. Different formulas work for different businesses/audiences.

### Framework 2: The Landing Page Optimization System

**Fuente:** "LPO System" (Kim, 2020)
**Propósito:** Optimizar landing pages para conversion.
**Cuándo usar:** Continuous improvement de landing pages.

**The 5-Element LPO Framework:**

**Element 1: Headline-Search Match**

**Rule:** Headline MUST include the keyword searched.

**Example:**
- Keyword: "best seo services for small business"
- Headline: "Best SEO Services for Small Businesses"

**Impact:** Reduces bounce rate by 30-50%, increases Quality Score.

**Element 2: Value Proposition Above Fold**

**Rule:** Communicate value before scroll.

**Framework:**
- Headline: What you offer
- Subheadline: Why it matters
- Bullet points: Key benefits (3-5)
- CTA: What to do next

**Element 3: Social Proof Cluster**

**Types:**
- **Testimonials:** Customer quotes with photos
- **Stats:** "Trusted by 10,000+ businesses"
- **Logos:** Client logos, certifications, awards
- **Case studies:** "How Company X achieved Y"

**Placement:** Above fold, next to CTA.

**Element 4: Single, Focused CTA**

**Rule:** One primary action, no competing CTAs.

**Effective CTAs:**
- "Start Free Trial"
- "Get Your Free Quote"
- "Download the Guide"
- "Book Your Consultation"

**Placement:**
- Above fold (visible immediately)
- Sticky (follows user on scroll)
- End of content (after reading value)

**Element 5: Form Optimization**

**Best practices:**
- **Fields:** Fewer is better (3-5 fields maximum)
- **Required:** Mark clearly, minimize required fields
- **Trust:** Include privacy note, security badges
- **Submit button:** Action-oriented ("Start Free Trial" not "Submit")

### Framework 3: The Campaign Scaling Framework

**Fuente:** "Scaling System" (Kim, 2021)
**Propósito:** Escalar campaigns profitability.
**Cuándo usar:** Cuando campaigns son profitability.

**The 3-Phase Scaling:**

**Phase 1: Validation ($100-500/month)**

**Goal:** Prove profitability

**Actions:**
- Test 50-100 keywords
- Test 5-10 ads
- Test 2-3 landing pages
- Identify winners

**Success criteria:**
- CPA < target
- ROAS > 2.0
- Consistent conversions (5+/week)

**Phase 2: Optimization ($500-2000/month)**

**Goal:** Maximize efficiency

**Actions:**
- Kill losers (bottom 50% of keywords)
- Scale winners (top 20% of keywords)
- Optimize landing page
- Expand ad variations

**Success criteria:**
- CPA < 50% of target
- ROAS > 3.0
- Scaling ready (30+ conversions/month)

**Phase 3: Scaling ($2000-10000+/month)**

**Goal:** Maximize growth while maintaining profitability

**Actions:**
- Horizontal scaling (new campaigns, new markets)
- Vertical scaling (increase budget on winners)
- Diversification (new ad platforms, remarketing)
- Automation (smart bidding, scripts)

**Success criteria:**
- Stable CPA despite scaling
- ROAS maintained > 2.5
- Sustainable growth

**Key Rule:** Don't skip phases. Scaling unvalidated campaigns = amplifying losses.

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The Click Quality Spectrum

**Fuente:** "Click Quality" (Kim, 2020)
**El modelo:** No todos los clicks son iguales.

**The Spectrum:**

**Low-Quality Clicks:**
- **Source:** Broad keywords, vague ads
- **Characteristics:** High bounce rate, low time on site
- **Result:** Low conversion rate (0-1%)
- **Economics:** Even low CPC is too expensive

**Medium-Quality Clicks:**
- **Source:** Medium-tail keywords, specific ads
- **Characteristics:** Moderate bounce rate, some engagement
- **Result:** Medium conversion rate (1-3%)
- **Economics:** Profitable at moderate CPC

**High-Quality Clicks:**
- **Source:** Long-tail keywords, qualified ads
- **Characteristics:** Low bounce rate, high engagement
- **Result:** High conversion rate (5-10%)
- **Economics:** Can afford higher CPC, still profitable

**Key Insight:** 100 high-quality clicks > 1000 low-quality clicks.

**Action:** Optimize for click quality, not just click quantity. Use ad copy to qualify, landing pages to convert.

### Modelo Mental 2: The Keyword Intent Matrix

**Fuente:** "Intent Matrix" (Kim, 2021)
**El modelo:** Keywords have different purchase intent.

**The Matrix:**

**Informational Intent:**
- **Keywords:** "what is", "how to", "why", "guide"
- **Intent:** Learning, researching
- **Conversion Rate:** Low (0.5-1%)
- **Strategy:** Content marketing, SEO over PPC

**Navigational Intent:**
- **Keywords:** Brand names, specific products
- **Intent:** Looking for specific site
- **Conversion Rate:** Medium (2-4%)
- **Strategy:** Bid on own brand, competitors' brands

**Commercial Investigation:**
- **Keywords:** "best", "vs", "review", "comparison"
- **Intent:** Comparing options
- **Conversion Rate:** Medium-High (3-5%)
- **Strategy:** Comparison content, strong differentiation

**Transactional Intent:**
- **Keywords:** "buy", "price", "cheap", "deal"
- **Intent:** Ready to purchase
- **Conversion Rate:** High (5-10%)
- **Strategy:** Aggressive bidding, strong offers

**Key Insight:** Allocate budget based on intent. 60% transactional, 30% commercial investigation, 10% brand.

### Modelo Mental 3: The Ad Auction Economics Model

**Fuente:** "Auction Economics" (Kim, 2020)
**El modelo:** Understanding SEM economics is critical.

**The Economics Formula:**

**Revenue = Clicks × Conversion Rate × Average Order Value**

**Cost = Clicks × CPC**

**Profit = Revenue - Cost**

**Key Insight:** There are 3 ways to improve profitability:

**Option 1: Increase Conversion Rate**
- Best leverage point
- 2x conversion rate = 2x profitability (same CPC)
- Action: Landing page optimization

**Option 2: Increase Average Order Value**
- Second best leverage
- 2x AOV = 2x profitability
- Action: Upsells, bundles, pricing strategy

**Option 3: Decrease CPC**
- Lowest leverage point
- 50% less CPC = 50% more clicks (but not 50% more profit)
- Action: Quality Score improvement, bid optimization

**Key Insight:** Most advertisers focus on CPC (Option 3), but Conversion Rate (Option 1) and AOV (Option 2) have higher impact.

**Action:** Sequence: Improve Conversion Rate first, then AOV, then CPC.

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### DC-1: ¿Cuándo usar broad match vs exact match?

**Broad match:**
- **When:** Discovering new keywords, low budget testing
- **Pros:** Finds opportunities, cheaper CPC
- **Cons:** Low relevance, high waste
- **Budget:** 10-20% of total

**Exact match:**
- **When:** Proven keywords, high budget campaigns
- **Pros:** High relevance, predictable performance
- **Cons:** Limited reach, higher CPC
- **Budget:** 70-80% of total

**Recommendation:** Start with phrase match for testing. Move to exact match for winners.

**Source:** "Match Type Strategy" (Kim, 2021)

### DC-2: ¿Cuándo usar single keyword ad groups (SKAGs)?

**Use SKAGs when:**
- High-value keywords (top 20% of conversions)
- Need granular control over bidding/copy
- Sufficient budget ($500+ per keyword/month)

**Don't use SKAGs when:**
- Limited budget (SKAGs fragment budget too much)
- Long-tail keywords with low volume
- Starting new campaigns (broader ad groups preferred)

**Recommendation:** Use SKAGs for top 10-20 keywords. Use broader ad groups for long-tail.

**Source:** "SKAG Strategy" (Kim, 2020)

### DC-3: ¿Cuándo remarketing campaigns?

**Use remarketing when:**
- Have 1000+ site visitors/month
- Sales cycle >7 days (consideration phase)
- Want to increase conversion rate from 2% to 4-6%

**Don't use remarketing when:**
- Low site traffic (<500 visitors/month)
- Impulse products (same-day purchase)
- Limited budget (focus on acquisition first)

**Recommendation:** Start remarketing after acquisition campaigns are profitable.

**Source:** "Remarketing Strategy" (Kim, 2021)

## 5. Anti-patrones

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 1: Focusing on CTR instead of conversion rate**
- **El problema:** High CTR ads often have low conversion rate (too generic)
- **El costo:** Many clicks, few conversions, negative ROI
- **la solución:** Focus on conversion rate first, CTR second
> *Fuente: "CTR Trap" (Kim, 2021)*

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 2: Sending all traffic to homepage**
- **El problema:** Generic homepage doesn't match search intent
- **El costo:** High bounce rate, low Quality Score, poor performance
- **la solución:** Dedicated landing pages per ad group/keyword theme
> *Fuente: "Homepage Mistake" (Kim, 2020)*

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 3: Not testing landing pages**
- **El problema:** Assuming landing page is good enough
- **El costo:** Missing 2-5x improvement in conversion rate
- **la solución:** A/B test landing pages continuously
> *Fuente: "Landing Page Neglect" (Kim, 2021)*

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 4: Bidding on competitor brand terms**
- **El problema:** Bidding on "HubSpot CRM", "Salesforce CRM"
- **El costo:** Very high CPC, low conversion rate (users searching competitor brand)
- **la solución:** Focus on category terms ("best CRM software"), not competitor brands
> *Fuente: "Competitor Bidding" (Kim, 2020)*

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 5: Scaling before validation**
- **El problema:** Increasing budget on campaigns not yet proven profitable
- **El costo:** Amplifying losses instead of profits
- **la solución:** Prove profitability at small scale before scaling
> *Fuente: "Premature Scaling" (Kim, 2021)*
