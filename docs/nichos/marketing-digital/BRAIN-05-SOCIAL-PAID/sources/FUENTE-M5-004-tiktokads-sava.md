# FUENTE-M5-004: TikTok Ads Academy — Akvile Sava

## Tesis Central

> TikTok no es Instagram con videos más cortos. El modelo "Native-First Advertising" significa: tu anuncio debe parecer contenido orgánico de TikTok, no un anuncio. Los users de TikTok punishing ads que feel like ads. La clave del éxito es "edutainment": education + entertainment. Tu anuncio debe entretener (stop the scroll) Y educar (deliver value) en los primeros 3 segundos. TikTok's algorithm es democratized: si tu contenido es bueno, TikTok lo muestra a miles, even con zero followers. La diferencia entre Facebook y TikTok: Facebook usa targeting, TikTok uses creative.

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: TikTok's algorithm es creative-first, no audience-first**
- Facebook: Targeting defines who sees your ad
- TikTok: Creative performance defines who sees your ad
- Good creative = organic reach, even with small budget
> *Fuente: "TikTok Algorithm Explained" (Sava, 2022)*

> **P2: La regla del 3-second drop-off**
- 50% de users drop off antes de second 3
- Si no captás attention en 3s, el rest doesn't matter
- El hook must be in the first 1-2 seconds
> *Fuente: "3-Second Hook Rule" (Sava, 2022)*

> **P3: Lo "cringe" funciona, lo "polished" falla**
- TikTok users reward authenticity over production
- UGC-style content outperforms polished ads
- Lo que look like ad = instant skip
> *Fuente: "Authenticity Over Polish" (Sava, 2021)*

> **P4: El audio es 50% del TikTok experience**
- Silent viewing is rare on TikTok
- Trending audio can boost organic reach
- Music/sound choice is critical for engagement
> *Fuente: "Audio-First Platform" (Sava, 2022)*

> **P5: TikTok's learning phase es más rápido que Facebook**
- TikTok optimiza en 24-48 hours (vs 72h+ for Facebook)
- Podés test y scale más rápido
- Pero ad fatigue también happens faster
> *Fuente: "TikTok Learning Speed" (Sava, 2022)*

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The Native-First Creative System

**Fuente:** "Native-First Advertising" (Sava, 2022)
**Propósito:** Crear ads que parezcan contenido orgánico de TikTok.
**Cuándo usar:** Al diseñar creativos para TikTok Ads.

**The 3 Elements of Native TikTok Content:**

**Element 1: The Authentic Hook (0-2 seconds)**

Your opening frame must:
- **Jump straight into action:** No intro, no logo, no "hey guys"
- **Show, don't tell:** Demo the problem or solution immediately
- **Use native TikTok language:** POV, storytime, "you won't believe this"
- **Text overlay bold:** 3-5 words max, big font, contrasting color

**Examples of Native Hooks:**
- "POV: You just discovered this hack that saves 2 hours/day"
- "Stop scrolling if you hate [problem]"
- "The [industry] secret nobody talks about"
- "I tried [product] for 30 days and here's what happened"

**Element 2: Edutainment Body (2-50 seconds)**

Deliver value through entertainment:

**Format A: The Problem-Solution Story**
- Show the problem (relatable struggle)
- Introduce your product as solution
- Demo how it works (real-time or before/after)
- Show the transformation/results

**Format B: The Listicle/Hack**
- "5 [category] hacks that changed my life"
- "3 mistakes you're making with [problem]"
- "[Number] things I wish I knew before [experience]"

**Format C: The Behind-the-Scenes**
- Show your process/creation
- Tour your office/setup
- Day in the life (with product integration)

**Element 3: The Native CTA (Last 3-5 seconds)**

TikTok-native call-to-actions:
- "Link in bio" (for organic content)
- "Swipe up" (for ads with landing page)
- "Follow for more [content type]"
- "Comment [keyword] for [resource]"

**Key Rule:** If your ad looks like a traditional TV commercial, it will fail on TikTok. Native TikTok content is raw, authentic, and unpolished.

### Framework 2: TikTok Ad Structure Blueprint

**Fuente:** "TikTok Ad Formats Guide" (Sava, 2022)
**Propósito:** Elegir el formato correcto de TikTok Ads.
**Cuándo usar:** Al configurar campaigns en TikTok Ads Manager.

**Format 1: In-Feed Video (Most Common)**

**Best for:** Awareness, consideration, conversions

**Specs:**
- Length: 9-15 seconds (optimal), up to 60 seconds
- Format: Vertical (9:16)
- Style: Native, authentic, entertainment-first
- CTA: "Shop Now", "Learn More", "Download", "Sign Up"

**Winning elements:**
- Fast cuts (every 1-2 seconds)
- Trending audio or sounds
- Text overlays for context
- Native transitions (not smooth TV-style)

**Performance benchmarks:**
- View-through rate (3s): > 25%
- CTR: > 2%
- CPV (Cost per View): <$0.02

**Format 2: Spark Ads (Amplified Organic Content)**

**Best for:** Leveraging organic UGC or influencer content

**Specs:**
- Uses existing TikTok posts (organic content)
- Appears as "Sponsored" but keeps native feel
- Can boost your own posts or others' posts (with permission)

**Advantages:**
- Maintains organic social proof (likes, comments)
- Leverages content that's already proven
- Lower production costs (reuse organic content)

**Strategy:**
1. Post organic content to test performance
2. Identify posts with high organic engagement
3. Boost top performers with Spark Ads

**Format 3: TopView (Brand Takeover)**

**Best for:** Massive reach, brand awareness

**Specs:**
- Full-screen takeover when user opens TikTok
- Length: 3-5 seconds (extended up to 60s with click-through)
- Frequency: One user sees it once per day

**Advantages:**
- Guaranteed impressions (purchased by CPM)
- Premium placement (first thing users see)
- High impact for brand campaigns

**Considerations:**
- Expensive (premium CPMs)
- Best for big brands with large budgets
- Requires high-quality, brand-safe creative

**Format 4: Brand Takeover (Similar to TopView)**

**Best for:** Product launches, major announcements

**Specs:**
- Full-screen static image or 3-second video
- Appears when user opens TikTok
- Click-through leads to landing page or hashtag challenge

**Format 5: Hashtag Challenge (Branded Trends)**

**Best for:** UGC generation, viral reach

**Specs:**
- Create a branded hashtag
- Users create content using your hashtag
- TikTok promotes the challenge in Discover tab

**Strategy:**
1. Create a catchy, easy-to-replicate hashtag
2. Launch with initial content (influencers, brand account)
3. Promote with in-feed ads to jumpstart participation
4. Engage with and reward user-generated content

**Key Rule:** Start with In-Feed Video ads (most versatile). Only consider premium formats (TopView, Brand Takeover) for big brand campaigns with large budgets.

### Framework 3: The TikTok Testing Framework

**Fuente:** "Rapid Testing Methodology" (Sava, 2022)
**Propósito:** Testear creatives rápido en TikTok.
**Cuándo usar:** Al lanzar nuevas campañas de TikTok Ads.

**Phase 1: The Creative Sprint (Week 1)**

Launch 20-30 creatives with:
- Budget: $10-20/day per creative
- Objective: Traffic or Video Views
- Duration: 3-4 days per creative

**What to test:**
- Different hooks (POV, storytime, listicle, BTS)
- Different lengths (9s, 15s, 30s, 60s)
- Different styles (talking head, demo, UGC-style)
- Different audio (trending vs custom vs instrumental)

**Kill criteria:**
- View-through rate (3s) < 20%
- Zero engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- CTR < 1%

**Keep criteria:**
- View-through rate > 30%
- High engagement relative to impressions
- CTR > 2%

**Phase 2: The Optimization Phase (Week 2)**

Take top 5-10 performers from Phase 1:
- Increase budget to $50-100/day
- Change objective to Conversion (if applicable)
- Test different audiences (broad vs interests vs lookalike)

**What to optimize:**
- First 3 seconds (hook refinement)
- Audio (test trending vs custom)
- CTA placement and wording
- Landing page (if applicable)

**Phase 3: The Scaling Phase (Week 3+)**

For validated winners:
- Scale budget 20-30% every 2 days
- Create variations of winning creatives
- Expand to new audiences geographically

**Scaling limit:**
- Stop scaling if CTR drops > 30% from baseline
- Stop scaling if CPA increases > 50% from baseline
- Create new variations instead of pushing same creative past limit

**Key Rule:** TikTok's algorithm learns FAST. You can test and scale in weeks, not months like Facebook. But this also means ad fatigue happens faster.

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The TikTok Algorithm (For You Page)

**Fuente:** "TikTok Algorithm Explained" (Sava, 2022)
**El modelo:** TikTok's "For You" page algorithm es very different de Facebook.

**How TikTok's Algorithm Works:**

**Phase 1: The Initial Test (First 100-200 views)**
- TikTok shows your content to a small test audience
- Monitors: Watch time, completion rate, engagement
- If initial performance is good → Phase 2
- If initial performance is bad → content dies here

**Phase 2: The Expanded Test (1,000-10,000 views)**
- TikTok shows content to more users
- Tests different audience segments
- If performance holds up → Phase 3
- If performance drops → content stops being shown

**Phase 3: The Viral Push (10,000-1,000,000+ views)**
- TikTok shows content to massive audience
- Content can go viral (millions of views)
- Duration: Hours to days, not permanent

**Key Factors TikTok Measures:**
1. **Watch time:** How long users watch (longer is better)
2. **Completion rate:** How many watch to the end
3. **Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares, saves
4. **Re-watch rate:** How many users watch multiple times

**Key Insight:** Unlike Facebook (where targeting determines reach), TikTok shows content based on PERFORMANCE. If your creative is good, TikTok will show it to thousands, even with zero followers.

**Implication for Ads:** Create ad creatives that could work organically. If users like your ad, TikTok's algorithm will reward it with lower costs and more reach.

### Modelo Mental 2: The Native Content Spectrum

**Fuente:** "Native vs Non-Native Content" (Sava, 2021)
**El modelo:** Hay un espectro de qué tan "native" es tu contenido en TikTok.

**Level 1: Non-Native (TV Commercial Style)**
- Characteristics: Polished, produced, scripted
- Logo upfront, product hero shot
- Callouts like "buy now!"
- User perception: This is an ad → SKIP
- Performance: Poor

**Level 2: Semi-Native (Instagram Style)**
- Characteristics: Good quality, aesthetic
- Influencer showing product
- Slightly scripted but natural
- User perception: This might be an ad → MAYBE WATCH
- Performance: Average

**Level 3: Native (TikTok Style)**
- Characteristics: UGC-style, authentic
- Jump straight into value
- Native TikTok language and format
- User perception: This is content → WATCH
- Performance: Good

**Level 4: Hyper-Native (TikTok Native)**
- Characteristics: Indistinguishable from organic
- Raw, unfiltered, authentic
- Trending audio, native transitions
- User perception: This is GREAT content → ENGAGE
- Performance: Excellent

**Key Insight:** The more native your ad, the better it performs. But native doesn't mean low quality. It means fitting TikTok's culture and format.

### Modelo Mental 3: The TikTok Funnel Accelerator

**Fuente:** "TikTok Funnel Dynamics" (Sava, 2022)
**El modelo:** TikTok acelera el funnel: awareness → consideration → conversion puede pasar en seconds.

**The TikTok Funnel:**

**Second 0-3: Awareness**
- User stops scrolling (hook works)
- User starts watching (value delivered)
- Metric: View-through rate (3s)

**Second 3-15: Consideration**
- User continues watching (content is interesting)
- User engages (likes, comments, shares)
- Metric: Engagement rate, watch time

**Second 15-60: Conversion**
- User clicks CTA (link in bio, swipe up)
- User visits landing page or takes action
- Metric: CTR, conversion rate

**Key Difference from Facebook:**
- Facebook: Awareness → Consideration → Conversion (takes days/weeks)
- TikTok: All 3 stages can happen in ONE 15-second video

**Implication:** Your TikTok ad must work as a full-funnel experience. Don't assume users will see multiple ads before converting. One ad can do it all.

**Action:** Design TikTok ads that include:
- Hook (awareness)
- Value/demo (consideration)
- CTA (conversion)
ALL in 15-60 seconds.

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### DC-1: ¿Cuándo usar TikTok Ads vs other platforms?

**Usá TikTok Ads cuando:**
- Tu target audience is Gen Z or Millennials (16-34)
- Tu product works well en video demos (fashion, beauty, food, apps, games)
- Tu brand can be authentic (not overly corporate)
- Tenés ability to create native-style video content

**No uses TikTok Ads cuando:**
- Tu target audience is older (45+)
- Tu product is B2B or complex enterprise software
- Tu brand requires ultra-polished, corporate image
- No tenés capacity for video production

**Source:** "Platform Selection Criteria" (Sava, 2022)

### DC-2: ¿Cuándo usar Spark Ads vs In-Feed Ads?

**Usá Spark Ads cuando:**
- Tenés organic content con high engagement
- Querés maintain social proof (likes, comments)
- Working with influencers (boost their content)
- Content is already proven (lower risk)

**Usá In-Feed Ads cuando:**
- Creating new ad creatives (not organic content)
- Need more control over targeting and optimization
- Running direct response campaigns (conversions)
- No existing organic content to leverage

**Source:** "Ad Format Selection" (Sava, 2022)

### DC-3: ¿Cuándo invertir en trending audio vs custom audio?

**Usá trending audio cuando:**
- Goal es awareness and organic reach
- Audio fits your brand and message
- Trend is in Phase 2 (trending but not saturated)
- Content is timely and relevant

**Usá custom audio cuando:**
- Necesitas specific messaging (voiceover, music)
- Audio is critical for your product (music brand, app)
- Building brand identity (consistent audio branding)
- Trending audio doesn't fit your content

**Source:** "Audio Strategy Framework" (Sava, 2022)

## 5. Anti-patrones

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 1: Repurposing Facebook/Instagram ads for TikTok**
- **El problema:** TV-style polished ads look out of place on TikTok
- **El costo:** Instant skips, low engagement, high CPA
- **La solución:** Create native TikTok content (UGC-style, authentic, unpolished)
> *Fuente: "Cross-Platform Repurposing" (Sava, 2022)*

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 2: Long intros or logos upfront**
- **El problema:** "Hey guys, welcome back to my channel!" = instant skip
- **El costo:** Lose viewers in first 3 seconds
- **la solución:** Jump straight into value. No intro, no logo upfront.
> *Fuente: "Intro Fatigue" (Sava, 2021)*

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 3: Ignoring the audio**
- **El problema:** Creating TikTok ads without considering sound
- **El costo:** Miss out on trending audio boost, lower engagement
- **La solución:** Design with audio in mind (trending sounds, music, voiceover)
> *Fuente: "Silent Treatment" (Sava, 2022)*

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 4: Treating TikTok like Facebook (targeting-heavy)**
- **El problema:** Spending hours on detailed targeting when creative matters more
- **El costo:** Wasted time, minimal performance impact
- **la solución:** Focus 80% on creative, 20% on targeting. TikTok is creative-first.
> *Fuente: "Facebook Mindset" (Sava, 2022)*

> **❌ ANTI-PATTERN 5: One-and-done creative testing**
- **El problema:** Testing 1-2 creatives and declaring "TikTok doesn't work"
- **El costo:** Giving up before finding winning combinations
- **la solución:** Test 20-30 creatives minimum. TikTok requires volume to find winners.
> *Fuente: "Insufficient Testing" (Sava, 2021)*
