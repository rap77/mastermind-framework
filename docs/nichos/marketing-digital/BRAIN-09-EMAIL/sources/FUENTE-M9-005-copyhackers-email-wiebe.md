# FUENTE-M9-005: Conversion Copywriting for Email (Joanna Wiebe - Copyhackers)

## Tesis Central

> **"El email copy que convierte no es sobre writing creativo — es sobre transcribir la VOZ del CUSTOMER. Si usas SUS palabras, SUS problemas, SUS aspiraciones en tu email, vas a convertir."**

---

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: La mejor copy viene de customer research, no de tu cerebro**
- Don't sit in a room y guess qué "sounds good". Go to your customers. Grab their words. Use THEM in your email. That's how you write converting copy.
> *Fuente: Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 1: "Voice of Customer Research" (Wiebe, 2017)*
> *Contexto: Antes de escribir CUALQUIER email, revisa testimonials, reviews, sales calls.*

> **P2: El email tiene UN JOB — don't give it 5**
- Un email puede sell, o nurture, o educate. Don't try to do all 3. Pick ONE job y exe Cute it brillantemente.
> *Fuente: Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 2: "Single-Objective Emails" (Wiebe, 2017)*
> *Contexto: Si tu email tiene 3 CTAs, rewrite. One CTA por email.*

> **P3: El subject line es HEADLINE, no summary**
- Don't summarize your email en el subject line. That's boring. Use the subject line como HEADLINE que creates curiosity o urgency.
> *Fuente: Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 3: "Subject Lines as Headlines" (Wiebe, 2017)*
> *Contexto: Treat subject line como newspaper headline, no como email summary.*

> **P4: El opening hook is make-or-break**
- Tienes 3 seconds. If tu opening don't hook, el email goes to trash. The best hooks are: (1) Story, (2) Surprising statement, (3) Question about their pain.
> *Fuente: Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 4: "Opening Hooks" (Wiebe, 2017)*
> *Contexto: Spend 50% of your time en subject line + opening hook.*

> **P5: La voz de tu brand es consistent, o es ruido**
- Si tus emails suenan diferentes cada vez — a veces formal, a veces casual, a veces salesy — nobody knows qué esperar. Consistency builds familiarity. Familiarity breeds trust.
> *Fuente: Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 5: "Voice and Tone Guidelines" (Wiebe, 2017)*
> *Contexto: Create voice guidelines y follow them en EVERY email.*

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The 4×2 Email Framework (Copyhackers Signature)

**Fuente:** Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 2: "The 4×2 Framework" (Wiebe, 2017)

**Estructura probada para emails que convierten:**

**The 4 Elements:**
1. **The Hook** — Grab attention en first 3 seconds
2. **The Problem** — Agitate their pain (show you understand)
3. **The Solution** — Present your offer as the answer
4. **The CTA** — One clear, specific action

**The 2 Psychology Triggers:**
1. **Urgency** — Why act NOW (not tomorrow)
2. **Objection Handling** — Address why they shouldn't buy

**Cómo aplicarlo:** Cada email debe tener los 4 elements + al menos 1 psychological trigger.

---

### Framework 2: The VOICE of Customer Extraction

**Fuente:** Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 1: "Voice of Customer Research" (Wiebe, 2017)

**Cómo obtener customer words:**

**Source 1: Reviews/Testimonials**
- Look for exact phrases customers use
- Ej: "This saved me 2 hours every day" → Use in email

**Source 2: Sales Calls/Support Tickets**
- What questions do they ask repeatedly?
- What objections do they raise?
- Ej: "I'm worried about X" → Address en email

**Source 3: Social Media Comments**
- What complaints do you see?
- What features do they praise?
- Ej: "Finally, a tool that does X" → Use en subject line

**Source 4: Competitor Reviews**
- What do people hate about competitors?
- Ej: "I hate that [competitor] doesn't have X" → Highlight your X

**Cómo aplicarlo:** Create a "customer words bank" document. Pull from it para every email.

---

### Framework 3: The Subject Line Formula Library

**Fuente:** Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 3: "Subject Line Formulas" (Wiebe, 2017)

**Copyhackers' probadas formulas:**

1. **The "How I" Formula**
   - "How I went from X to Y in Z days"
   - Ej: "How I went from 0 to 10K subscribers in 3 months"

2. **The "Stop Doing X" Formula**
   - "Stop doing X if you want Y"
   - Ej: "Stop sending 'just checking in' emails if you want replies"

3. **The "The Truth About" Formula**
   - "The truth about [common belief] nobody tells you"
   - Ej: "The truth about 'passive income' nobody tells you"

4. **The "X Ways to Y" Formula**
   - "[Number] ways to [achieve result] without [pain point]"
   - Ej: "7 ways to grow your list without paid ads"

5. **The "You Don't Need" Formula**
   - "You don't need [X] to [Y] (here's what works instead)"
   - Ej: "You don't need a big budget to launch (here's what works instead)"

**Cómo aplicarlo:** Test al menos 2 formulas por email campaign.

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The Customer is the Hero, Not Your Brand

**Fuente:** Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 5: "Hero-Centric Copy" (Wiebe, 2017)

**Concepto:** Tu brand es el GUIDE. El customer es el HERO. Don't say "We're the best". Say "You can achieve X".

**Implicación práctica:** Replace "we/our" con "you/your" throughout all email copy.

---

### Modelo Mental 2: The Email is a Continuation, Not a Start

**Fuente:** Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 2: "Sequences vs. One-Offs" (Wiebe, 2017)

**Concepto:** Each email builds on previous emails. Don't treat each email como isolated. The sequence creates the narrative arc.

**Implicación práctica:** Plan sequences in advance. Each email should reference previous content or set up future content.

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### Trade-off 1: Long vs. Short Copy

| Long Copy (500+ words) | Short Copy (200 words) |
|----------------------|----------------------|
| ✅ Mejor para high-ticket | ✅ Mejor para low-ticket |
| ✅ Más storytelling depth | ✅ Higher completion rate |
| ❌ Lower read rate | ❌ Menos persuasive power |

**Decisión:** Long copy para first purchase. Short copy para repeat purchases.

**Fuente:** Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 4: "Copy Length Testing" (Wiebe, 2017)

---

## 5. Anti-patrones

### Anti-patrón 1: The "We Are Excited" Opening

**Síntoma:** Starting emails with company-centric language.

**Fix:** Start with customer problem or story.

**Fuente:** Copyhackers Email Copywriting Course, Module 4: "Opening Hooks" (Wiebe, 2017)

## Métricas Clave

- **Open Rate:** Benchmark against industry (20% is avg)
- **Click-Through Rate:** 3-5% es good, 5%+ es excellent
- **Conversion Rate:** 1-3% es avg, 3%+ es excellent
- **Unsubscribe Rate:** < 0.5% per send es healthy
