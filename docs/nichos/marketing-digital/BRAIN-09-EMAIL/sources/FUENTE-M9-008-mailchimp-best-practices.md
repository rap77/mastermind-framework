# FUENTE-M9-008: Email Marketing Best Practices (Mailchimp)

## Tesis Central

> **"El email marketing es UNA PARTE de tu marketing mix, no todo. Integration con otros channels (social, web, ads) es clave. El email que mejor funciona es el que se integra con el customer journey COMPLETO."**

---

## 1. Principios Fundamentales

> **P1: La lista size matters less que list quality**
- 1,000 engaged subscribers valen más que 100,000 cold leads. Focus en engagement, no en vanity metrics como "total subscribers".
> *Fuente: Mailchimp 2023 Benchmark Report, "List Quality vs. Quantity" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)*
> *Contexto: Clean your lists regularmente. Remove inactives.*

> **P2: Mobile-first es mandatory, no optional**
- 50-70% de emails se abren en mobile. Si tu email no se ve bien en mobile, estás losing half tu audience.
> *Fuente: Mailchimp 2023 Benchmark Report, "Mobile Optimization" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)*
> *Contexto: Test todos tus emails en mobile antes de enviar.*

> **P3: El send time depende de TU audience, no de industry averages**
- "Best time to send email" es un myth. The best time = cuando TU audience opens. Test y encuentra TU sweet spot.
> *Fuente: Mailchimp 2023 Benchmark Report, "Send Time Optimization" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)*
> *Contexto: A/B testea send times con tu propia lista.*

> **P4: La segmentación es el #1 predictor de success**
- Segmented campaigns tienen 14.31% higher open rates que non-segmented. La data es clear: segmenta o perderás.
> *Fuente: Mailchimp 2023 Benchmark Report, "Segmentation Impact" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)*
> *Contexto: Segmenta por location, engagement, purchase history, interests.*

> **P5: El A/B testing es ongoing, no one-time**
- No testees una vez y done. Test CONTINUAMENTE. Subject lines, send times, content, CTAs — testea todo, siempre.
> *Fuente: Mailchimp 2023 Benchmark Report, "A/B Testing Culture" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)*
> *Contexto: Cada campaign es una oportunidad para aprender.*

## 2. Frameworks y Metodologías

### Framework 1: The Mailchimp Segmentation Matrix

**Fuente:** Mailchimp Best Practices Guide, "Segmentation Strategies" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)

**Segmentation types por effectiveness:**

| Segment Type | Open Rate Lift | Difficulty |
|--------------|---------------|------------|
| **Engagement** (Active vs Inactive) | +40% | Easy |
| **Purchase History** (Buyers vs Non-buyers) | +30% | Easy |
| **Location** (Geo-based) | +15% | Easy |
| **Sign-up Source** (Lead magnet type) | +20% | Medium |
| **Email Client** (Mobile vs Desktop) | +10% | Medium |
| **Behavioral** (Clicks specific link) | +35% | Hard |
| **Predictive** (Likelihood to buy) | +50% | Hard (requires ML) |

**Cómo aplicarlo:** Empieza con engagement segmentation (fácil y high impact). Progresa hacia behavioral segmentation.

---

### Framework 2: The A/B Testing Framework

**Fuente:** Mailchimp Best Practices Guide, "A/B Testing Playbook" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)

**Qué testear en orden de impacto:**

**1. Subject Lines (Highest Impact)**
- Test: Curiosity vs. Question vs. Urgency
- Winner: 20-30% open rate difference
- Sample size: Min 1,000 subscribers per variant

**2. From Names (Surprising High Impact)**
- Test: Company name vs. Person name vs. Combo ("Sarah from Company")
- Winner: 10-15% open rate difference
- Insight: People open emails from PEOPLE, not companies

**3. Send Times (Medium Impact)**
- Test: Morning (9am) vs. Afternoon (2pm) vs. Evening (6pm)
- Winner: Varies por audience (test for yourself)
- Note: Different industries have diferentes optimal times

**4. Content Format (Lower Impact)**
- Test: Plain text vs. HTML
- Test: Short vs. Long copy
- Test: Single image vs. Multiple images
- Winner: Depends en audience preference

**Cómo aplicarlo:** Test one variable at a time. Never test subject line AND send time simultáneamente.

---

### Framework 3: The Mobile-First Design Checklist

**Fuente:** Mailchimp Best Practices Guide, "Mobile Optimization" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)

**Checklist para mobile-friendly emails:**

**Layout:**
- [ ] Single column layout (no multi-column on mobile)
- [ ] Font size min 14px (16px ideal para body)
- [ ] CTA buttons min 44x44px (tap-friendly)
- [ ] Preheader text optimized (preview en mobile)

**Images:**
- [ ] Images compressed (< 50KB per image)
- [ ] Alt text en ALL images (si images don't load)
- [ ] Responsive images (scale down en mobile)

**Content:**
- [ ] Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- [ ] Bulleted lists para escaneabilidad
- [ ] Key points en **bold**
- [ ] Most important CTA "above the fold"

**Technical:**
- [ ] Media queries implemented (CSS para mobile)
- [ ] Tested en iOS (Gmail app) y Android
- [ ] Dark mode compatible (colors invert en dark mode)

**Cómo aplicarlo:** Usa mailchimp's mobile preview tool antes de cada send.

## 3. Modelos Mentales

### Modelo Mental 1: The Email Lifecycle (Not Static List)

**Fuente:** Mailchimp Best Practices Guide, "List Management" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)

**Concepto:** Tu email list es un living ecosystem. Subscribers enter (new signups), engage (active), churn (inactive), and sometimes return (win-back).

**Implicación práctica:** Manage the ENTIRE lifecycle, not just acquisitions. Re-engage inactives BEFORE removing them.

---

### Modelo Mental 2: The Campaign Integration (Email is ONE Channel)

**Fuente:** Mailchimp Best Practices Guide, "Multi-Channel Integration" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)

**Concepto:** Email no opera en vacuum. Integrate con social media (share buttons), website (signup forms), y ads (retargeting).

**Implicación práctica:** Cada email debe have social share buttons. Email subscribers should get exclusive social content. Ads should retarget email engagers.

## 4. Criterios de Decisión

### Trade-off 1: HTML vs. Plain Text (Revisit Again)

| HTML Email | Plain Text |
|-----------|-----------|
| ✅ Visual appeal | ✅ More personal |
| ✅ Tracking capabilities | ✅ Higher deliverability |
| ✅ Brand consistency | ✅ Simpler to create |
| ❌ Spam risk (if poorly coded) | ❌ No tracking |
| **Best for:** B2C, ecommerce, newsletters | **Best for:** B2B, personal outreach |

**Decisión:** Mailchimp data muestra que HTML emails have 10% higher CTR promedio. Test con tu audience.

**Fuente:** Mailchimp Best Practices Guide, "HTML vs. Plain Text" (Mailchimp Team, 2023)
